Alligator River National Wildlife Refuge Celebrates Anniversary

Date: August 5, 2024

Description: Alligator River National Wildlife Refuge, located in Tyrrell County, commemorates its 40th anniversary since establishment. The refuge, spanning over 152,000 acres, serves as a critical habitat for a diverse range of wildlife species, including black bears, red wolves, and migratory birds. In celebration of this milestone, the refuge is hosting special events, guided tours, and educational programs to raise awareness about conservation efforts and biodiversity conservation.
