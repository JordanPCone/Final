Public Safety Services

Tyrrell County provides comprehensive public safety services to its residents. The Tyrrell County Sheriff's Office is responsible for law enforcement and maintaining public order within the county. Emergency medical services are provided by Tyrrell County Emergency Medical Services (EMS), ensuring rapid response to medical emergencies. The county also maintains fire departments that respond to fires, accidents, and other emergencies.

In addition to emergency response, Tyrrell County Emergency Management Agency (EMA) coordinates disaster preparedness, response, and recovery efforts in the event of natural or man-made disasters, ensuring the safety and well-being of residents.

Codes and Laws

Tyrrell County enforces various codes and laws to maintain public safety, health, and welfare. Zoning regulations govern land use and development, ensuring orderly growth and protecting property values. Building codes set standards for construction, ensuring that structures are safe and comply with building standards.

The County also enforces animal control ordinances to address issues related to pet ownership, and environmental regulations to protect natural resources and mitigate environmental hazards. Residents and businesses can access information about these codes and laws through the County's website or by contacting relevant departments.

Jobs and Employment

Tyrrell County offers employment opportunities in various fields, providing meaningful career opportunities for its residents. Job openings may include positions in administration, public safety, public works, parks and recreation, social services, and healthcare.

Job openings and application procedures are typically posted on the County's website and may also be advertised through local newspapers and job boards. Interested individuals are encouraged to apply online or through the County's Human Resources Department.

Parks and Recreation

Tyrrell County maintains parks, recreational facilities, and programs to promote active lifestyles and community engagement. Parks offer a variety of amenities, including sports fields, playgrounds, walking trails, picnic areas, and pavilions.

The County also organizes recreational programs and events for residents of all ages, such as youth sports leagues, fitness classes, outdoor concerts, and community festivals. These programs provide opportunities for socialization, physical activity, and cultural enrichment.

Permits and Records

Tyrrell County issues various permits and maintains records related to construction, land use, business operations, and vital statistics. Building permits are required for new construction, renovations, and alterations to existing structures.

Business licenses are required for operating businesses within the county, ensuring compliance with local regulations and generating revenue for the county. The County also maintains vital records, including birth certificates, death certificates, and marriage licenses, which are essential documents for legal and administrative purposes.

Recycling and Garbage

Tyrrell County provides recycling and garbage collection services to residents and businesses, promoting environmental sustainability and waste reduction. The County operates recycling centers where residents can drop off recyclable materials such as paper, cardboard, glass, plastics, and metals.

Garbage collection services are provided on a scheduled basis, with designated pick-up days for different areas of the county. Residents are encouraged to separate recyclable materials from non-recyclable waste to minimize landfill usage and conserve natural resources.

Volunteering Opportunities

Tyrrell County offers volunteering opportunities for individuals interested in giving back to their community. Volunteer opportunities may include assisting with community events, serving as mentors for youth programs, participating in environmental clean-up efforts, and providing support for senior services.

Volunteers play a vital role in enhancing the quality of life in Tyrrell County, contributing their time, skills, and expertise to support various County programs and initiatives. Individuals interested in volunteering can inquire about opportunities through the County's Volunteer Services Department.
